Welcome to Sarah's Page

Hi, I'm Sarah Boyle and I am a Junior at Lehigh University. I am currently double majoring in Journalism and Politcal Science. I am also a member of Zeta Tau Alpha sorority, where I am extremely invovled. In the past, I have been my chapter's Global Union Liaison, Director of Social Events and Recruitment Sisterhood Chair. I was recently elected to be ZTA's Panhellenic Delegate. On campus, I am a reporter for Lehigh's newspaper, The Brown and White. Some of my other interests are traveling, fashion and blogging.
